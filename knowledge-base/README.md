## What Phoxta is (one line)
An **AI-native multi-business platform**: it builds complete, ready-to-launch businesses on one shared backend, packages each as a **cloneable blueprint**, sells those blueprints to many buyers in a marketplace, and **matches** the founders, operators and investors behind them.

> "We will not sell you a website. We will sell you a business that works, with the AI inside, with the automations on, with the operations already running — and we will keep working to make it better, with you." — *Customer Charter*

## The four moves (operating rhythm)
1. **Create** — spin up a tenant from a template in minutes (zero marginal cost per new design).
2. **Build** — site, content, products, AI assistants, automations (a real working business).
3. **Operate** — run it semi-autonomously to prove unit economics buyers inherit.
4. **List & sell** — publish as a blueprint; clone it to many buyers (one design, many sales).

## Five revenue streams (Year-5 mix)
Blueprint clone fees (35%) · Platform subscription (30%) · Usage-based AI add-ons (15%) · Matching & facilitation (10%) · Marketplace take rate (10%).
