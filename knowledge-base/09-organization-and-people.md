# 09 — Organization & People

## Team & leadership

- **Founder & CEO** — 12+ yrs multi-tenant platforms + production customer-facing AI; led eng at a SaaS platform scaled 0→50k+ customers; shipped LLM features to hundreds of thousands daily; ran real e-commerce/content businesses; content-led GTM + founder-led sales.
- **Co-founder & CTO** — infra/ML/DX; led platform eng at high-growth SaaS; Postgres at scale + RLS; production ML serving; OSS multi-tenant Postgres maintainer; built teams 5→60+.
- **Co-founder & Head of Product** — operator + design thinking; ran product 0→1 and 1→10; 500+ hrs SMB customer interviews; ran a marketing agency 3 yrs.

**Founding team (joining at Seed close):** Head of Engineering (Platform), Head of AI, Senior frontend engineer, Senior brand/product designer; Head of Marketing (Q4 2026).

**Advisory board (4 domains):** multi-tenant platform architecture · AI infrastructure & governance · marketplace economics & operations · SMB customer acquisition.

## Organizational design
**Principles:** small teams/big mandates · function over hierarchy · async by default · eat your own dogfood · hire for slope not Y-intercept.

| Function | Yr1 (18) | Yr3 (~71) | Yr5 (~193) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Engineering | 10 | 30 | 70 |
| Product + design | 3 | 8 | 18 |
| GTM (S+M+CS) | 3 | 25 | 85 |
| G&A | 2 | 8 | 20 |

**Remote-first** with 3 optional hubs (London, NYC, Singapore); written-first; quarterly in-person weeks; annual offsite; home-office stipend; 4+ hr time-zone overlap.

**Compensation:** cash at 75th percentile of home market · above-median equity for early team, refresh every 2 yrs · minimal bonus · comprehensive benefits · twice-yearly reviews; promotions any time · published salary bands.

## Hiring plan
Hire deliberately to a **need** (clear mandate + qualified pipeline + 24-mo runway), not to a plan. First 18 months: Head of Eng (Platform) + Head of AI (mo1), senior FE + designer (mo2), Head of Marketing (mo3), backend AI + mobile (mo4), content writer (mo5), backend core + senior PM (mo6), FE marketplace (mo8), CS lead + Head of Ops (mo10), staff engineer (mo12), eng manager (mo14), first AE (mo16), Head of CS (mo18).

**Funnel quality bar:** applied 100% → resume ~25% → screen ~50% → assessment ~50% → onsite ~40% → offer close ~70%.
**DEI:** 50% of sourced candidates from underrepresented groups; ≥1 underrepresented candidate per senior slate; quarterly conversion review; annual comp-equity review.
**Onboarding (60 days):** setup/security/shadowing → domain immersion + first small project + buddy → first solo medium project → day-60 documented review.

## Culture in practice
> "Culture is the sum of decisions we make when the obvious thing is uncomfortable."

Values show up concretely: **Clarity** (written agendas 24h ahead, decision logs, <2-page plans, no strategy decks); **Ownership** (anyone can ship, DRI per project, blameless postmortems); **Compounding** (platforms not features, sacred component library, pay debt every sprint); **Calibration** (publicly grade OKRs quarterly, confidence levels in planning); **Trust by default** (public channels, public RFCs, visible salary bands); **Customer obsession** (everyone talks to a customer monthly, eng rotates support 1 wk/quarter).

**Rituals:** weekly all-hands (Fri 30m) · monthly company review · quarterly OKR retros · quarterly in-person weeks · annual offsite (Sept) · Friday "whoops" demo.
**Won't tolerate:** disrespect · metric manipulation/dishonesty · brilliant assholes · status games · info hoarding.
**Celebrate:** "I don't know" said confidently · respectful public disagreement with seniors · killing a non-working project · unprompted documentation · cross-team help · early error-calling.

## Matching service (Founder, Co-founder & Investor)
The matching layer turns a marketplace of businesses into an **ecosystem of teams**. Four surfaces:
1. **Co-founder matching** — profile/ML matching (skill/capital/commitment/vertical/location); success = formed partnership within 90 days; **$500 success fee**; Yr2 target 500+ profiles, 100+ matches.
2. **Operator-for-hire** — vetted operators matched to opportunities; **10% of first-year comp escrowed 12 mo**; Yr2 100+ profiles, 30+ placements.
3. **Investor group** — group formation + Phoxta-blessed SPV; **$2,500 setup + 1% of capital + admin**; Yr3 30+ SPVs, $5M+ deployed.
4. **Advisor matching** — vertical advisors booked by customers; **15% take on session fees**; Yr3 300+ advisors, 5,000+ sessions.

**Trust mechanisms:** profile verification, reputation across surfaces, mediation/dispute resolution, code of conduct, privacy (no contact share without mutual opt-in), 90-day operator satisfaction guarantees.
**Why hard to copy** (vs CoFoundersLab/FounderDating): matching around **concrete opportunities** not abstract profiles; shared operating environment; **verified business performance**; multi-sided dynamics.
