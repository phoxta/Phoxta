# 05 — Go-to-Market & Brand

## Marketing strategy
**Philosophy:** earn customers through demonstrated value, not interruption — proof, narrative, matchmaking; every dollar measured for incremental ARR.

**Five pillars:** (1) Content as proof (blueprints are the marketing — live demos, tear-downs, unit-economics breakdowns); (2) Community for distribution (participate in existing communities); (3) SEO & AI visibility (rank/answer "how to start a [vertical] business"); (4) Strategic partnerships (resellers, accelerators, investor networks); (5) Founder-led narrative.

**Marketing mix (Yr1 → Yr2 budget):** Content+SEO $80k→$240k · Community $40k→$80k · Paid search $30k→$120k · Paid social $25k→$100k · Partnerships $20k→$80k · PR/thought leadership $30k→$60k · Events $25k→$80k. **Total $250k → $760k.**

**Funnel:** Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Purchase → Onboarding → Expansion → Advocacy (each with tactics + KPIs, e.g., branded search volume, marketplace sessions, saved blueprints, conversion rate, activation rate, NRR, referrals/NPS).

**Year-1 milestones:** brand + phoxta.com w/ 10 guides (Q3'26); 3 blueprint case studies; marketplace beta + 5 launch partners (Q4'26); founder podcast circuit (15 shows); 100k monthly organic visitors; 50 paying buyers + 10 stories; first reseller program (Q2'27).

## Brand strategy & positioning
**Positioning statement:**
> For people who want to **own a real business — not assemble one** — Phoxta is the AI-native platform that sells ready-to-launch businesses, cloned to your name, on a single shared backend that scales with you. Unlike SaaS toolkits, business brokers, or starter templates, Phoxta delivers working AI-enabled businesses with documented economics and matches you with the people you need to grow.

- **Archetypes:** The Builder (confident craft) + The Magician (complex made effortless).
- **Voice:** Direct, not aggressive · Specific, not vague (numbers > adjectives) · Smart, not academic · Warm, not corporate. No hype words ("revolutionary", "unleash").
- **Visual identity:** palette **deep navy, gold, indigo, teal**; confident serif display + humanist sans body; real workspaces/people/screens (no stock clichés); generous whitespace, typography-led; subtle/purposeful motion.

**Brand promises:** won't sell a tool when you need a business · tell you unit economics before you buy · keep your business yours (data, customers, right to leave) · won't sell to two competitors in the same city (geographic exclusivity on premium) · continuously improve the platform for you.

**Anti-brand (what Phoxta is NOT):** website builder · no-code platform · business broker · MLM/dropshipping/get-rich-quick · consultancy/agency · AI chatbot company.

### Competitive positioning one-liners
- Shopify/WordPress (tools to build) → we sell **working businesses**, not toolkits.
- Empire Flippers/Flippa (existing businesses) → we sell **new clones with documented architecture**, not lemons.
- ThemeForest/template stores → we sell **live, AI-enabled, operating systems**.
- McKinsey/agencies (custom builds) → same quality at **5–10% of the price**.
- Search funds/EFGs → we are the **deal-flow + operating-platform layer underneath**.
- Generic AI agents → our AI is **in the foundation**, governed and metered.

## Sales strategy
**Charter:** "Sales velocity is a function of clarity… We do not push. We illuminate."

**Two motions:** (1) **PLG** for blueprints <~$3k and Starter/Growth — full self-serve in marketplace; (2) **Founder/sales-assisted** for premium, multi-blueprint, Enterprise, investor groups (team of 2 in Yr1 → 5 in Yr2; founder on largest deals).

**Sales targets:** Yr1 50 customers / $1,800 avg / $0.3M ARR; Yr2 400 / $2,500 / $3.5M; Yr3 1,500 / $3,200 / $12M; Yr4 3,500 / $3,800 / $28M; Yr5 6,000 / $4,200 / $52M. Per-AE quota $800k (Yr2) → $1.8M (Yr5).

**Tooling:** Phoxta's own CRM (dogfood), live multi-blueprint demo console, buyer deal-room, per-vertical onboarding playbooks, AI sales assistant.

## Customer journey & lifecycle (7 buyer stages)
Discovery → Exploration → Evaluation → **Purchase** (one-click clone + Stripe, ~5 min) → **Onboarding** (30-day concierge, first revenue) → **Running** (Months 2–12, target first-year retention >85%) → **Growth/Advocacy** (Yr2+, 30%+ expand within 12 months).

**End-customer journey** (the buyer's customers, who never know Phoxta exists): branded domain → fast ISR pages → semantic search/recommendations → checkout via buyer's payment account → post-purchase automations + AI support → behavioral events feed analytics.

**Retention levers:** onboarding excellence (−60% first-90-day churn), feature adoption, health scores (catch churn 60 days early), community + peer learning (+10–15%), continuous auto-applied platform improvements, customer council (20 customers shape roadmap).

## Content & community strategy
**Content pillars:** (1) Vertical deep-dives ("How to start a [vertical] business in 2026", 5–10k words, 1/month); (2) Tear-downs (operating model + economics, 1/2 weeks + podcast); (3) Founder stories (numbers, like *Acquired*); (4) Building Phoxta in public (engineering posts, quarterly metrics); (5) The macro view (founder essays, 1/quarter).

**Community:** Year 1 — participate in existing communities (Indie Hackers, Hacker News, Acquire.com, Search-Fund/EFG forums, local SMB groups, conferences). Year 2 — launch a Phoxta-customer-only community (per-vertical forums, monthly office hours, cohort programs, annual Founders' Summit).

**Content team:** Yr1 Head of Content + 1 senior writer + 0.5 SEO contractor → Yr2 +2 writers, video/podcast producer, community manager, full SEO.
